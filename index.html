I built all of this for myself first — not because I'm a tech person, but because I was drowning in my own life and needed a way out. Now I want to explore this with others. Aurora Journey is a shared exploration: small group calls where we trade ideas, honest conversations about what actually works, and your feedback shaping where we go next. I don't have all the answers — but I have a path, and it's better walked together.

This is a shared journey, not a polished product. We'll meet on calls, share what's working, inspire each other with real use cases — and your feedback will shape what Aurora becomes.
